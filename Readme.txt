## General Information
This dataset is from a Cafe operating in a Microsoft company located in china from 2012-01-01 to 2015-09-19. It contain different factors that influence sales , holidays, average weather, outdoor activities and weekends.

- What problem does it (intend to) solve?
We are trying to gather quantity sales and how sales are distributed.



- What is the purpose of your project?
The aim is to provide insight for the caf√©'s strategic decisions, increasing productivity during busy times and minimising potential drops in sales.

- Why did you undertake it?
To learn more about the cafe and how transactions are made, and what customers prefer.
